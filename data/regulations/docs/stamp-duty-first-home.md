SECTION: First Home Buyers Assistance Scheme (FHBAS)

Eligible first home buyers in NSW receive full or partial transfer duty exemption under the First Home Buyers Assistance Scheme:

- New or existing home up to $800,000: full duty exemption.
- New or existing home $800,000 to $1,000,000: concessional rate,   tapering to zero at the threshold.
- Vacant land up to $350,000: full exemption; $350,000 - $450,000:   concessional.

Eligibility requires the buyer to be an Australian citizen or permanent resident, aged 18 or over, never previously owned residential property in Australia (including investment), and intend to occupy the home within 12 months for a minimum continuous 6 months. Joint purchasers must all qualify.
