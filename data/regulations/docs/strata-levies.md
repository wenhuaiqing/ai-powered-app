SECTION: Strata levies and special levies

Owners corporations must set two annual levies at each AGM under ss79-80 of the Strata Schemes Management Act 2015:

- Administrative Fund levy: covers day-to-day running costs (insurance,   utilities for common property, management fees, minor maintenance).
- Capital Works Fund levy (formerly sinking fund): for major repairs and   capital expenditure over the 10-year capital works plan.

Levies are apportioned by unit entitlement and payable quarterly unless otherwise resolved. Unpaid levies attract default interest (10% p.a. by statute unless otherwise resolved) and can be recovered in the Local Court within 6 years of the due date. Special levies for unforeseen expenses require a general meeting resolution.
