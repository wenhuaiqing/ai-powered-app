SECTION: Transfer duty (stamp duty) rates

Transfer duty in NSW is calculated on a sliding scale based on the dutiable value (greater of purchase price or market value) of residential property. Approximate brackets (subject to annual indexation):

- Up to $17,000: $1.25 per $100 (min $20)
- $17,001 - $36,000: $212 + $1.50 per $100 over $17,000
- $36,001 - $97,000: $497 + $1.75 per $100 over $36,000
- $97,001 - $364,000: $1,564 + $3.50 per $100 over $97,000
- $364,001 - $1,212,000: $10,909 + $4.50 per $100 over $364,000
- $1,212,001 - $3,636,000: $49,069 + $5.50 per $100 over $1,212,000
- Above $3,636,000 (premium): $182,389 + $7.00 per $100 over $3,636,000

Worked example: on a $900,000 purchase, duty is approximately $10,909 + 4.50% × ($900,000 − $364,000) = $10,909 + $24,120 = $35,029. Duty is payable within 3 months of contract date.
