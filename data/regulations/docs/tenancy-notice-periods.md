SECTION: Notice periods to end tenancy

Notice periods (s84 - s90 RTA 2010) depend on who ends the tenancy and why:

- Tenant ending a fixed-term tenancy without grounds: 14 days notice if   given on or after the end date, otherwise pay break-fee equal to 4   weeks (≤25% of fixed term remaining), 3 weeks (25-50%), 2 weeks   (50-75%) or 1 week (75-100%) of rent.
- Tenant ending a periodic tenancy: 21 days notice.
- Landlord ending a periodic tenancy without grounds: 90 days notice   (reformed in 2024 — no-grounds termination is being phased out).
- Landlord ending for sale of premises: 30 days notice with contract   attached.
- Landlord ending for non-payment of rent (≥14 days arrears): 14 days   termination notice.
- Domestic violence circumstances: tenant may end immediately, no   break-fee.
