SECTION: Buyer disclosure (section 184 certificate)

Before buying into a strata scheme, a purchaser is entitled to inspect the strata roll and request a section 184 Certificate. The certificate lists the unit entitlement, current levies and arrears, balance of the administrative and capital works funds, insurance details, by-laws, minutes of recent meetings, and any current Tribunal orders.

The fee for a s184 certificate is currently capped at $43.40 (CPI indexed). The owners corporation must provide the certificate within 14 days of payment. Buyers should also consider commissioning a strata search (broader review of books and records by a strata reporting company) before exchanging contracts.
