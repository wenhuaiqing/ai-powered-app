SECTION: Water usage charges

Landlords may only pass on water USAGE charges (not fixed service charges) to tenants if all three conditions are met: the premises are separately metered; the premises meet the water-efficiency standards (specified maximum flow rates for showerheads, taps, dual-flush toilet cisterns); and all leaks are repaired.

Bills must be passed on within 3 months of the landlord receiving the bill from the water utility. The tenant is to pay only the usage portion (measured kilolitres × rate), not the daily service availability fee. Where premises are not separately metered, no usage charge can be passed on regardless of any contrary term in the tenancy agreement.
